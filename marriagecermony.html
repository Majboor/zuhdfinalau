Frequently Asked Questions

In what language is the marriage service conducted?

The service is available in English by default. However, you may request to receive it in Arabic only or in both English and Arabic. I don’t speak other languages, but I’d be happy to explore options if you require support in a different language.

What do I talk about?

You don’t need to say anything if you’re feeling too nervous—just being present is enough. Whether or not I include words of inspiration or advice for the bride and groom during the ceremony introduction depends on the tone and style you’d like for your wedding.

Where can the marriage service be held?

The ceremony can be conducted in English, Arabic, or a combination of both—based on your preference. While I don't speak other languages, I'm happy to explore alternative arrangements if you require the service in a different language.

How long does the ceremony go for?

The ceremony typically lasts up to 25 minutes, but it can be customized to be shorter or longer based on your needs and preferences.
