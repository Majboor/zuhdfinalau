What is the importance of Surah Al-Fatiha in daily prayers?
Surah Al-Fatiha is the opening chapter of the Quran and is recited in every unit of Salah. It holds great significance as a prayer for guidance and mercy.

The Quran is believed to be the final and complete message of Allah, revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) as guidance for all of humanity.

What does the Quran say about treating parents?
The Quran emphasizes kindness to parents, especially in old age, as stated in Surah Al-Isra (17:23), "Say not to them a word of disrespect."

What is the meaning of Taqwa in the Quran?
Taqwa refers to consciousness of Allah, being mindful of His commands and avoiding what He forbids. It is a central theme throughout the Quran.

What are the benefits of reading Surah Al-Kahf on Fridays?
Reading Surah Al-Kahf on Fridays brings light for the whole week and protects from the trials of Dajjal, according to Hadith and scholarly interpretation.

What is the reward of reading the Quran?
